## Section 7: Summary

HHOC's IT support in 2025 was delivered through a lean, fully remote model: 149.1 hours across 169 tickets, supported by 18 technicians with a blend of US-based and offshore resources. Total 2025 revenue was $12,192 — one of Technijian's smaller managed services engagements.

At $12,192 total spend for ~10 users (8 desktops, 9 mailboxes), the per-user IT cost is approximately **$1,219/year**. This covers fully managed desktop security, email protection, monitoring, patching, and support — with zero one-time charges and zero overage.

**Key Findings:**

- **Email & M365 Dominance:** 41.4% of tickets and 46.4% of hours are concentrated in Email & M365. This is the single most impactful area for optimization — reducing email-related issues through user training, standardized configurations, or self-service tooling would have the largest effect on overall ticket volume.

- **Fully Remote Model:** 100% remote support with zero onsite visits or drive time. This is efficient and appropriate for HHOC's lean desktop-and-email environment.

- **Growing Demand:** H2 hours (89.4) were 50% higher than H1 (59.7), with Q4 being the busiest quarter. Average ticket complexity also increased over the year (0.57 hrs/ticket in Q1 to 1.07 in Q4). This trajectory should be monitored entering 2026.

- **Clean Billing Profile:** Zero overage, zero one-time charges, and 100% predictable monthly billing. The quarterly labor adjustment cycle effectively right-sizes allotments to actual demand.

- **Nonprofit Efficiency:** As a nonprofit social services organization, HHOC benefits from M365 Non-Profit pricing and the cost efficiency of the offshore-heavy support model. The overall blended labor rate of $48.42/hr delivers comprehensive coverage at an accessible price point.

- **US Tech Support Dependency:** Sunny Sark as the sole US tech resource (26% of all hours) provides strong account continuity but creates a coverage risk. A designated backup US resource should be identified.

- **Contract Right-Sizing:** The April step-up (from ~$660/month to ~$1,000+/month) and November NH rate reduction ($30 to $15) demonstrate active contract management on a quarterly cycle. Labor allotments are well-matched to actual demand.
