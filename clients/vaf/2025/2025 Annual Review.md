## Section 7: Summary

VAF's IT support structure is comprehensive and well-functioning. The model delivers 333.9 hours of support across 462 tickets annually, with 7-day coverage and deep expertise across cloud infrastructure, security, patching, VoIP, and end-user support.

At $117,229 total spend for 50 managed users (48 desktops, 8 cloud servers), the per-user IT cost is approximately **$2,345/year**. This covers fully managed cloud hosting, security, backup, monitoring, telephony, network management, and 24/7 support.

The ticket categorization reveals a healthy proactive/reactive balance: **66.7% of tickets are proactive** (patching, monitoring, security, backups, RMM). The primary optimization opportunities are:

- **Environment Stabilization:** Ticket volume declined significantly from January (69) to a steady ~30–35/month by mid-year, indicating the managed environment has matured. The declining hours-per-ticket trend (0.95 to ~0.63) confirms growing efficiency.
- **Coverage Model Efficiency:** The blended labor rate of $27.90/hr reflects very strong offshore leverage — 93% of billed hours are offshore, keeping costs extremely low while maintaining 24/7 coverage.
- **Quarterly Labor Right-Sizing:** The 3-month contract cycle effectively adjusts labor allotments to match utilization, declining from ~$2,231/month in January to ~$470–$522/month by year end as the environment stabilized.
- **Patch Automation:** 210 tickets (45.5% of all work) in patching. Reducing patch failure rates through better automation would meaningfully reduce ticket volume.
- **Infrastructure Investment:** The $19,931 office recabling and $4,650 3CX upgrade were significant one-time investments that modernize the physical and telephony infrastructure for years to come.
- **Hardware Lifecycle:** No desktop replacements occurred in 2025. Establish a rolling replacement budget ($3K–$5K/yr) to address the aging fleet proactively.

We are confident that with these adjustments, VAF will enter 2026 well-supported and operating efficiently.
