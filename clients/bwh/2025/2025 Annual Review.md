## Section 1: Support Scope & Coverage

### 1.1 What Is Included in Monthly Support

The monthly agreement covers a comprehensive managed IT package for BWH, totaling $95,464 across 2025 (~$7,955/month). This breaks down into two components:

**Labor (~$57,329/year, ~$4,777/month):** Offshore technical support provides coverage across US business hours ($30/hr, billed as offshore after-hours) and US overnight hours ($15/hr, billed as offshore normal hours). US-based technical support ($125/hr) handles escalations and onsite work. A Systems Architect advisory retainer ($200/hr, 5 hrs/month) provides strategic guidance.

**Managed Services (~$38,135/year, ~$3,178/month):** Security stack (CrowdStrike, Huntress, phishing training), monitoring (RMM, ManageEngine Ops Manager, patch management), backup (image backup, Veeam 365, cloud backup storage), email security (anti-spam, DKIM/DMARC), secure internet filtering, pen testing, and site assessment.

### 1.2 Coverage Hours

- **Total Hours Delivered:** 1,459 hours across 2,179 unique tickets in 2025.
- **US Overnight (Offshore NH):** 974 hours (66.7% of total) at $15/hr — this is the overnight monitoring and maintenance coverage delivered by the offshore team during their normal business hours (India daytime = US overnight).
- **US Business Hours (Offshore AH):** 479 hours (32.9% of total) at $30/hr — US daytime support delivered by the offshore team during their after-hours (India night shift).
- **Onsite:** 5.9 hours (0.4%) across 3 visits — QNAP disk replacement (May), onsite network work, and a December visit.
- **Weekend Coverage:** 659 time entries on Saturdays and Sundays (27.5% of all entries), confirming 7-day availability.
- **Staffing Depth:** 19 unique technicians, with 7 primary resources each contributing 100+ hours.

### 1.3 Remote vs. Onsite Mix

The model is 99.6% remote. Only 5.9 onsite hours were logged in 2025 (3 visits), with 6.0 hours of drive time. Onsite visits are reserved for hardware-related needs only (disk replacement, network equipment).

### 1.4 Services Billed Outside Monthly

- **Recurring Licensing ($16,636):** Cybertraining, cloud backup, anti-spam (transitioned from monthly billing in April), M365 backup, Sophos firewall subscription, edge appliance, and Entra ID P1.

- US Tech Support hours decreased from ~18.2 hrs/month to ~12.4 hrs/month (-32%)
- Offshore NH hours increased from ~37.5 hrs/month to ~51.6 hrs/month (+38%)
- Offshore AH hours increased from ~38.9 hrs/month to ~45.4 hrs/month (+17%)
- Rates remained unchanged across all roles
- Several service items (Anti-Spam, Phishing Training) moved from Monthly to Recurring billing
- Pen Testing was removed from the monthly invoice after July

This represents a cost optimization shift — moving labor from US-based ($125/hr) to offshore ($15–30/hr) while expanding the offshore team's coverage hours. The net effect increased total managed services while reducing per-hour labor costs.

**No fixed 3/6/12-month cycle was detected.** The adjustment appears to have been a one-time contract right-sizing rather than a periodic review. We recommend establishing a semi-annual review cadence going forward.

### 8.2 Support Cycle Analysis: Billed vs. Actual Hours

A critical input for 2026 projections is whether the current labor allotments match actual demand. The April/May 2025 contract adjustment shifted hours from US-based to offshore support. Analyzing the current cycle (May–Dec 2025) reveals significant misalignment:

**Billed vs. Actual Hours — Current Cycle (May–Dec 2025 Average)**

| Role | Billed Hrs/Mo | Actual Hrs/Mo | Utilization | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| IT Support ($125/hr) | 12.4 | 20.9 | **166%** | Under-provisioned |
| Offshore NH ($15/hr) | 51.6 | 60.2 | **115%** | Slightly under |
| Offshore AH ($30/hr) | 45.4 | 37.3 | **82%** | Over-provisioned |
| Systems Architect ($200/hr) | 5.0 | N/A (retainer) | — | Fixed retainer |

**Key Finding — IT Support is significantly under-billed.** The April contract adjustment reduced US Tech Support from ~18.2 to ~12.4 hrs/month, but actual demand averaged 20.9 hrs/month during the current cycle — a 166% utilization rate. Several months were extreme: August hit 31.8 actual hours (255% of allotment) and September hit 38.8 hours (312%). At $125/hr, the ~8.5 hrs/month shortfall represents approximately **$1,063/month ($12,750/year) in labor delivered above the contracted amount.**

Offshore NH hours are running slightly above contracted (115%), which is manageable at $15/hr. Offshore AH hours are over-provisioned at 82% utilization — the contract allots more US-daytime offshore hours than are being consumed.

*Note: October actual hours were depressed across all roles due to the late-September malware attack redirecting resources. Excluding October, the IT Support utilization rate would be even higher.*

**Recommended Right-Sizing for Next Contract Cycle**

The recommended allotments include a ~10% buffer above trailing averages. The net monthly labor increase would be approximately **+$1,417/month (+$17,000/year)**, driven almost entirely by right-sizing the US Tech Support allotment. The offshore AH reduction partially offsets the increases.
